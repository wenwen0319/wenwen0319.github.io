My name is Yunyu Liu. I will be a Ph.D. student at Purdue University.

My research interests are primarily using multi-view learning and semi-supervised learning for action recognition. Previously, I’m a member of SMILE Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Yun Raymond Fu. Currently I’m working on projects related to graph neural network.

Before going to NEU, I obtained my bachelor degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2018. In SJTU, I conducted some research on IoT, wireless networking using machine learning. To get more information about my research work, you can go to the publication page and projects page.
